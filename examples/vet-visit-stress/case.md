# Vet Visit Stress

Fictional user prompt:

```text
Use $cat-behavior-vet to assess this case:

My 8-year-old spayed indoor cat panics during car rides and veterinary visits.
She hides when the carrier appears, pants in the car, and sometimes urinates in
the carrier. The clinic has suggested a pre-visit medication, but I want to know
what evidence-based handling and preparation steps should come first.
```

Expected skill behavior:

1. Ask about medical status, visit urgency, respiratory signs, prior medication
   reactions, carrier history, and clinic handling.
2. Separate acute medical risk from behavior preparation.
3. Retrieve evidence on feline stress, handling, and pre-appointment medication.
4. Provide a plan for carrier training, transport setup, clinic coordination,
   and when medication should be discussed with the veterinarian.
